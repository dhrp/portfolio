Thatcher Ross Peskens

Thatcher Peskens is a User Experience Designer by day, and hacker/developer by night. Trained as an Industrial Design Engineer and Interaction Designer he understands how to manage design processes, even with complex requirements.

The past two years he has been on a mission to make collaboration in creative design teams easier. He founded Share Square, created a multi-disciplinary team and proved to get things done.

Background

Thatcher created his first website in 1999, even before he started Industrial Design. During his studies he developed a strong interest in designing software with a human-centric approach and worked on a number of websites and web applications. A masters in Design for interaction provided further experience with executing user research, concept testing and design research.

Delft University of Technology's design program has always been a strong driver for users-centric design, and graduates currently fulfill UX design positions with companies such as IDEO, Frog, Microsoft and Google.

Technology

Although not formally educated in programming his interest in creating compelling user interactions has brought him to develop actual coding skills in several environments including HTML5, CSS3, jQuery and ember.js (in which this website is built), C# (Silverlight) (in which Share Square was built), Python (Django) and AS3 (Adobe Flex).
