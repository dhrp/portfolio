Product concept for e-paper posters

Goal of the product Service Systems course was to come up with a new concept for a more sustainable product system using a service. We have developed a system in which E-paper displays placed in the city replace printed posters.

Research

Research For development of this product-service system we have done research to assess how and when the concept will be technically possible. Two students from industrial ecology, University of Leiden, participated and estimated the possible reduction. For more information see the full paper here (PDF 5,1Mb)
