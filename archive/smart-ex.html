Smart-eX Smart interactive fitness bike

For: Course ID4220 Interactive Technology Design

Goal of the course is to design a smart interface for a fitness bike.
Keywords are interaction, motivation and coaching.

We have developed and tested a system that through the use of "smart" "automatic" coaching motivates people to keep training over an extended period of time. Our target group is people recovering from knee injuries, and our vision distinct by motivating people by coaching instead of entertainment.
