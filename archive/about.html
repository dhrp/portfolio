About KOFFIEDIK industrial

‘koffiedik kijken’ (looking at coffee residue) is a dutch saying of ‘looking in the future’. In past times a fortuneteller would come by and drink coffee (hot water and ground-coffee mixed) with you. She or he would then tell your future by looking at the residue left over, in special patterns, in your cup.

KOFFIEDIK industrial has a vision and a philosophy to look into the future. One can give a few words and the company will know, or find out, what product or service is needed, and how it should be. Your idea is therefore in good hands.

KOFFIEDIK industrial is currently a one-man company, or maybe just another name for Thatcher Peskens. He currently studies Design for Interaction, a master of the Faculty Industrial Design Engineering of the well known Technical University Delft (TUDelft).

Besides his study he also likes to do other things, like organising a Festival (Wolpop), being involved in a student society (Wolbodo) working with linux-servers (currently 3) making websites, sports (rowing) and cooking nice food.
