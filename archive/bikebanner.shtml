Design of BikeBanner product for ads on bicycles



Introduction
For Co-Con product development KOFFIEDIK industrial has designed a product to place ads on bicycles. Through a full design process the final product has become a plastic board, which can be placed inbetween the spokes of a bicycle. The product is currently in production and sold internationally.



For more information see www.bikebanner.eu and www.co-con.nl

KOFFIEDIK industrial has also designed the brand-image of fietsmedia / bikemedia.
